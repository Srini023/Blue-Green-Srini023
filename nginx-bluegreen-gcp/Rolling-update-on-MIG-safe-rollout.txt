When you release a new version, you normally:

create a new template
apply rolling update to MIG

Example update Green MIG to new template:
gcloud compute instance-groups managed rolling-action start-update nginx-green-mig \
  --version=template=nginx-template-v2 \
  --max-surge=1 \
  --max-unavailable=0 \
  --zone=<ZONE>
