Two practical approaches:
Option 1: Rolling restart (if supported in your gcloud track)

gcloud compute instance-groups managed rolling-action restart nginx-green-mig \
  --zone=<ZONE>

Option 2 (works everywhere): Rolling replace (recreate instances one-by-one)

gcloud compute instance-groups managed rolling-action replace nginx-green-mig \
  --max-surge=1 \
  --max-unavailable=0 \
  --zone=<ZONE>
